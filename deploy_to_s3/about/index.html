The basic idea: trying to live a simple life and create beautiful things.

Andrew Ramos is an illustrator, designer and maker of things living in Greenville, SC. He’s a fan of beautiful pencils, thin lines, and smart remarks and has an insatiable desire to understand how things work. He enjoys seeing new places, and plotting world domination with the CoWork crew, but usually opts for a night at home with his amazingly creative wife, Elizabeth.
